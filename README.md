# cycle

*a quiet app for knowing yourself better*

---

Cycle is a personal period and phase tracker built for one person — designed to be calm, private, and genuinely useful rather than feature-heavy or clinical.

It lives at its own URL, syncs silently to the cloud, and works on any device without asking anything of you. No account to create. No subscription. No ads. No algorithm deciding what you need to see.

Just your cycle, your symptoms, and your patterns — over time.

---

## what it does

**Tracks your cycle** with phase-aware insights that know the difference between day 2 of menstrual and day 5. Because they feel different.

**Predicts upcoming periods** based on your real history — not a generic 28-day assumption. The more you log, the sharper it gets.

**Logs daily symptoms** across four categories — physical, energy, mood, and the good stuff — as color-coded pills you tap in seconds.

**Shows patterns over time** in an Insights tab that connects your symptoms to your phases, so you can start to see what your body has been trying to tell you for years.

**Syncs automatically** across devices via Supabase. Open it on your phone, your tablet, your laptop — it's always current.

---

## what it doesn't do

It doesn't track fertility or basal body temperature.

It doesn't send notifications or push you toward engagement.

It doesn't share your data with anyone. Your health information belongs to you.

---

## how it was built

Built entirely in conversation with [Claude](https://claude.ai) — designed, iterated, and refined over a single session. The data lives in [Supabase](https://supabase.com). The app is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com). No frameworks. No build tools. Just an HTML file and a vision.

---

## the history inside

The app launched pre-loaded with 21 cycles of real data imported from TempDrop — a baseline that gives the algorithm something real to work from from day one.
