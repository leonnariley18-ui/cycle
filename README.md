# cycle ✨

*a quiet app for knowing yourself better*

---

Built for one person. Designed around one body. Yours.

Cycle is a personal period and phase tracker that lives at its own URL, syncs silently to the cloud, and works beautifully on any device — no account to create, no subscription, no ads, no algorithm deciding what you need to see.

Just your cycle, your symptoms, and your patterns — building into something meaningful over time.

---

## what it does

**Knows where you are** — a phase-aware hero card greets you by time of day and tells you exactly where you are in your cycle. Tap it to reveal a full phase ring: four colored arcs proportional to your actual cycle lengths, a white marker on today, your cycle day at the center.

**Tracks your period** — log when it starts, record daily flow as it progresses, end it when it's done. The app remembers everything. A gentle nudge appears if you're past your predicted start date.

**Logs daily symptoms** — 19 symptoms across four categories, color-coded and tap-to-select. Physical, energy, mood, and the good stuff. Navigate back through the last 7 days without leaving the screen.

**Gives you phase-day insight** — not just "you're in luteal." Day 2 of menstrual reads differently from day 5. The app knows the difference and speaks to it.

**Predicts what's coming** — upcoming periods shown in Insights, with a quiet countdown that only appears when you're 7 days out. No noise before then.

**Builds patterns over time** — as you log, Insights begins to surface your personal patterns: which symptoms cluster to which phases, what your body tends to do and when. The data is yours and it gets richer with every cycle.

**Syncs automatically** — every tap saves instantly to the cloud via Supabase. Open on your phone, your tablet, anywhere — it's always current. PIN-protected so only you can access it.

---

## what it doesn't do

Fertility tracking. Basal body temperature. Notifications. Ads. Data sharing. Pink.

---

## the foundation

Launched with 21 cycles of real data imported from TempDrop — a baseline that gave the algorithm something true to work from on day one. Every cycle logged going forward makes the predictions sharper and the insights more personal.

---

## how it was built

Built entirely in conversation with [Claude](https://claude.ai) — designed, iterated, stress-tested, and refined across a single extended session. The data lives in [Supabase](https://supabase.com). The app is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com). No frameworks. No build tools. No app store. Just an HTML file, a vision, and a lot of intention.

---

## the design

Dark and moody. Soft indigo-purple background. Phase colors that shift with your body — rose for menstrual, mint for follicular, amber for ovulation, periwinkle for luteal. Lora serif for the moments that matter, DM Sans for everything else. Haptic feedback on every meaningful tap. A shimmer across the hero card every time you come home to it.

Built to feel like it belongs on your phone at 11pm. Because that's when you'll use it.
